Enable Microphone

One-time setup for voice recording in Snackbar.

1
Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar (top right)
2
Find Snackbar in the list and click the three dots menu next to it
3
Click "View web permissions"
4
Find Microphone and change it from "Ask" to "Allow"
5
Come back to this tab and click the button below to verify

This only needs to be done once. After granting access, you can close this tab.
